We invite you to an immersive adventure that starts 13th February in Holoseum - the biggest digital art space in Tbilisi, Georgia. You are going to have an exciting journey through several interactive artworks, that are designed to clean our minds from some of the stereotypes and prejudices. Digital technologies allow to create breathtaking imaginary environments that help us look at ourselves from different perspective, question some rigid patterns of our perception. Following that intention our team have crafted 8 multimedia artworks and we invite you to join this extraordinary experiment.
